Facials

Medical and Spa Facials

Our facials are customized and designed for any skin condition and types. They help reduce Pores, Spots, Fine Lines, and Wrinkles, and lift eyebrows and saggy contours.

The treatment includes enzyme peels, microdermabrasion, gentle extractions, massage, customized mask and oxygen infusion with vitamins and minerals. The results are lasting and require only a monthly or bi-monthly follow-up.

There is no downtime, no pain, no redness—just a beautiful, more youthful face with great-looking skin. A series of twelve sessions is recommended for the best results.

 

How Facials Work

Facials are a treatment for the face that’s meant to cleanse, firm, smooth and moisturize the skin.

Many people swear by facials not only as a way to feel relaxed and pampered, but also as a means for making their skin healthier.

Each person who gets a facial has his or her own reasons for doing so, including:

  • deeply clean the skin and pores
  • improve skin that is either too dry or too oily
  • rejuvenate skin that has begun to look old or wrinkled
  • relax and de-stress

At Svetlana’s Skin Secrets we offer you Medical and Spa facials, for men, women and teenagers. All our facials are designed to deliver healthier radiant skin.
